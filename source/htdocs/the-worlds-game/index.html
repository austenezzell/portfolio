The World's Game project started as an excuse to merge two of my passions; photography and soccer. The plan was to play soccer, every time I saw a pic-up game, and then take a picture of the field. On a trip through Asia the project began to have new meaning. I found that through mutual love of soccer, I could connect with people of completely different brackgrounds, cultures and languages.
